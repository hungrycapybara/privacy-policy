Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 8, 2025

Welcome to Hungry Capybara ("we", "our", or "us"). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application (“App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address and password to create and manage your account.
  • Usage Data: We track in-app activity, such as the number of coins you earn, game interactions, and time spent in the app. This helps us improve the user experience.
  • Withdrawal Information: When you request a withdrawal, you manually provide payment method details (e.g., mobile banking ID, wallet number, or other account details) to process your payment.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device identifiers (such as device model, operating system, and app version) to maintain app security and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Track coin earnings and process withdrawal requests.
  • Improve, personalize, and maintain our services.
  • Communicate with you about updates, withdrawals, or account issues.
  • Prevent fraudulent activity and ensure compliance with our terms.

3. Manual Withdrawals

All coin withdrawals are processed manually by our team based on the payment details you provide. Please ensure that your details are correct, as we are not responsible for failed transactions caused by incorrect or incomplete information.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating the app (bound by confidentiality agreements).
  • When required by law, legal processes, or government requests.
  • To protect our legal rights, prevent fraud, and maintain security.

5. Security of Your Information

We use reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee 100% protection.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

6.1 Account Deletion

You may request permanent deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time. Once your request is processed, all data related to your account will be permanently removed from our systems, except for any information we are legally required to retain (such as transaction records for tax or legal purposes).

To request account deletion, send an email to official.hungry.capybara@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account with the subject line “Account Deletion Request.”

Please note that account deletion is irreversible, and you will lose all coins, rewards, and associated data.

7. Children’s Privacy

Hungry Capybara is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover that a child has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Your Privacy Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Update or correct your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at official.hungry.capybara@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. You are encouraged to review this policy periodically.
